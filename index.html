🌾 About Annapurna AI

Annapurna AI is a smart agricultural assistant designed to empower farmers with data-driven decisions. Our platform provides personalized crop recommendations, real-time weather updates, and market insights to help farmers improve productivity and income.

Farmers often lack access to scientific soil data (NPK values) and market forecasts. Annapurna AI bridges this gap by using AI + open data sources (soil, weather, irrigation availability) to give reliable suggestions in a simple, farmer-friendly interface.

Our mission is to make farming smarter, sustainable, and profitable while ensuring accessibility in local languages for every farmer in India.

🌾 Crop Disease Detection

Early detection of crop diseases is the key to protecting farmer income and ensuring better yields. Our AI-powered Crop Disease Detection system allows farmers to simply upload a photo of their plant leaves and instantly receive insights about potential diseases and their severity.

Instead of waiting for visible damage or depending only on traditional methods, farmers can now use modern technology to get fast, reliable, and easy-to-understand results. The system also provides treatment suggestions and preventive measures to stop the spread and protect healthy crops.

This feature empowers farmers to take timely action, reduce crop loss, and increase productivity with the help of digital innovation.
